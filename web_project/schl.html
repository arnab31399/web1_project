ABOUT SCHOOL


WWA Cossipore English School is a Co-Education institution near Dum Dum with Indian Certificate of Secondary Education Affiliation in Kolkata. 2018-19 Application starts from Aug 1, 2017. Find fees structure , reviews and feedback. Age eligibility for LKG is 4 Yrs by 31st March.


'WWA Cossipore English School' is a school in North Kolkata, India, established in 1976. Affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, the school provides education from KG to Class 12. Students are offered the choice of two streams for High School - Science and Commerce.
